-- About me --

I'm Max Parata, a voxel artist, 3D artist and game developer. I'm also a member of the TORTOR collective, an awesome group of artist that make free assets just like me. Come visit our page to see all the good stuff we have :

-- What is monogon? --

monogon is a series of Premium Quality asset packs compatible with each other.  The models are optimized for games with low poly count and baked textures. The characters are skinned and fully compatible with mixamo by simply drag and dropping into the mixamo browser.  And a free sample content is provided for developers to try out the pack before buying.
